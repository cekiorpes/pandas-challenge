Observations on Heroes of Pymoli data analysis

1. Heroes of Pymoli players are largely male and spend the most as a group, but the female and other/non-disclosed gender players spend more money on average when playing.

2. The game is most popular with players age 15-30, with players aged 20-24 comprising the largest group. Most of the revenue comes from these groups, but players aged 35-39 have the highest average total purchase per person. 

3. The Final Critic and Oathbreaker items are the most popular as well as the most profitable items. None of the 5 items with the highest individual price were in the top 5 most popular or most profitable.
